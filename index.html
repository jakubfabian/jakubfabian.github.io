My main research focus is on the evolution of clouds and the role of radiation.

Cloud Visualization with MonteCarlo methods

Sophisticated, physically correct radiative transfer methods allow to visualize simulations of clouds.

3D Heating Rates for LES

I develop an accurate, yet fast and highly parallel finite volume solver to compute solar and thermal heating rates to drive atmospheric models.

Cloud modelling

I work with various high resolution models to examine the intricate relationship between radiation and the evolution and organization of clouds.
